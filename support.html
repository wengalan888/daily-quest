Help with Daily Quest

Daily Quest: Day Planner is a lightweight iOS app for creating reusable quests, organizing them into categories, picking tasks for today, completing them, and reviewing progress in a simple log.

Getting Help

If something is not working as expected, please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots are helpful when they do not contain private information.

For support, contact ProbablyTimid at probablytimid@gmail.com.

FAQ

What is a quest?

A quest is a reusable task or routine you may want to complete on more than one day, such as stretching, reading, cleaning, planning, or checking in on a habit.

How is Daily Quest different from a calendar app?

Daily Quest is for lightweight recurring daily planning. It is not meant for heavy project management, calendar scheduling, meeting coordination, or long-term timeline planning.

Where is my data stored?

Your user-created app data is stored locally on your device. Backup files are created only when you choose to export them.

Can I move my data to another device?

Use the app's export feature to create a backup file, then import that file on another device. Keep backup files somewhere secure because they may contain your personal task information.

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Check that notifications are enabled for Daily Quest in iOS Settings. Local reminders also depend on the reminder options you have set inside the app.

Do widgets update automatically?

Widgets are managed by iOS and may refresh on a system-controlled schedule. Opening the app can help refresh the latest task information.
