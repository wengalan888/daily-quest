Built for recurring routines

Keep today clear without turning life into a project board.

Daily Quest is designed for simple repeatable planning: small tasks, daily choices, and a light history of what you completed.

Reusable Quest Bank

Create quests once and keep them ready for future days. Organize them into categories so your routine stays easy to scan.

Pick Tasks for Today

Choose only the quests that fit your day. Daily Quest helps you focus on a short, intentional list instead of a crowded schedule.

Progress Log

Review completed tasks over time and notice the patterns that help your days feel more grounded.

Reminders

Set local reminders for important routines so your plan can gently meet you at the right time.

Backups

Export and import backup files when you want to move or protect your data. You stay in control of the files you create.

Widgets

Use widgets to keep your daily quests close by without opening the app every time you want to check your progress.

Private by design

Your plans belong to you.

Daily Quest does not require an account and is built around local device storage. Your quests, categories, daily selections, completion log, reminders, backup files, and widget data stay on your device unless you choose to export a backup.
