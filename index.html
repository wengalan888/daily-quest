Privacy Policy

Daily Quest · Last updated April 2026

We collect no data

Daily Quest does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you enter -- your quests, daily selections, and completion history -- is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device.

No third parties

Daily Quest does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with anyone, ever.

No account required

Daily Quest does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.
