How it works

Keep today clear without turning life into a project board.

Daily Quest is built for recurring routines and everyday planning without the weight of a full calendar or heavy task manager.

01

Create, edit, and organize quests once, then keep them ready in categories for the days when they fit.

02

Pick what fits today

03

Track progress lightly

Inside the app

See the daily loop in a few screens.

Daily Quest keeps the flow simple: maintain a reusable quest bank, choose the right quests for today, and review your progress later.

Quest Bank

Create reusable quests, group them into categories, and schedule them for every day or selected weekdays.

Today

Keep the day focused with a short list of selected quests and a clear view of what is done or still open.

Progress Log

Review completed and open quests in the log to spot patterns and keep a light record of your days.

More than a checklist

Built for routines that repeat.

Daily Quest supports local reminders, backup export and import, and Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets so the app stays useful without getting heavy.

It is designed for recurring life rhythms and everyday planning, not long-term project management or a full calendar.

Private by design

Your plans belong to you.

Daily Quest does not require an account and is built around local device storage. Your quests, categories, daily selections, completion log, reminders, backup files, and widget data stay on your device unless you choose to export or share a backup.
